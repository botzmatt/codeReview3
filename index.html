Let's Play!

Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number by 1s with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
Disclaimer: Inputting too large of a number may crash your browser.
